MANAGING DIRECTOR
Architectural thinking. Long-term ownership.

Christoforos Roungeris is an Architect Engineer and graduate of TU Delft's MSc Architecture, Urbanism & Building Sciences programme. Before joining APXR, he worked for over three years at Dam & Partners Architecten in Amsterdam, contributing to complex residential and mixed-use developments. Today, as Managing Director of APXR, he oversees the company's development, asset management and strategic growth. Building on a longstanding family real estate portfolio, his focus is on modernizing operations, unlocking value through thoughtful development, and creating assets designed for long-term performance. Combining architectural training with hands-on ownership and management experience, he approaches real estate as both a spatial and financial discipline.

Concept

Aether is designed as a restrained island house that prioritizes atmosphere over spectacle. The architecture frames the horizon while remaining grounded in the topography and material language of the Cyclades.

The project is organized around exterior living, sheltered thresholds and a direct relationship between interior rooms, terrace, pool and landscape. Its expression is intentionally quiet: stone, plaster, shade, water and light.

Approach

The Corfu Residence reflects the company’s long relationship with real estate as both an architectural and personal undertaking. Rather than treating renovation as a cosmetic exercise, the project focused on atmosphere, durability and the careful upgrading of an existing residence.

Its inclusion in the APXR portfolio marks the continuity between earlier family-led architectural work and the company’s current direction: improving assets with restraint, long-term thinking and respect for place.

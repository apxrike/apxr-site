Aether

Villa in Kythnos. A private Cycladic retreat shaped by horizon, wind, stone and shade.

Concept

Aether is designed as a restrained island house that prioritizes atmosphere over spectacle. The architecture frames the horizon while remaining grounded in the topography and material language of the Cyclades.

The project is organized around exterior living, sheltered thresholds and a direct relationship between interior rooms, terrace, pool and landscape. Its expression is intentionally quiet: stone, plaster, shade, water and light.

Philosophy
A lean, architecture-led development company with an operator’s eye for value.

APXR combines architectural training, development discipline and direct asset management experience. The company focuses on projects where design quality and financial logic reinforce each other.

Context before image

Each project begins with location, orientation, existing value and long-term use — not with a pre-packaged style.

Restraint over noise

We prefer calm, durable and materially grounded spaces over fashionable gestures that age badly.

Value through clarity

Good design is not decoration. It is a way to make assets easier to understand, stronger to occupy and better to hold.

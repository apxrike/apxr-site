What we do

APXR operates at the intersection of architecture, development and ownership. We focus on creating, improving and managing properties with a long-term perspective, combining design thinking with direct investment and active stewardship.

01

Development

Identifying, structuring and delivering carefully selected real estate opportunities, from early concept through implementation.

02

Transformation

Repositioning existing properties through architecture, renovation and strategic investment, unlocking value while respecting context and character.

03

Stewardship

Active ownership and long-term management focused on durability, performance and the sustained improvement of each asset.

How we work

Long-term thinking informs every decision, from acquisition and design through ownership and management.

We favour long horizons over quick returns. Architecture, development and ownership are treated as a continuous process rather than separate disciplines.

Projects are selected carefully and managed directly, with an emphasis on context, restraint and long-term value creation.

Whether developing new projects or improving existing assets, our objective remains the same: to create places that endure both spatially and financially.

Aether

Villa in Kythnos. A private Cycladic retreat shaped by horizon, wind, stone and shade.

LocationKythnos, Greece
TypologyPrivate villa
AreaApprox. 132 m² interior
StatusUnder development
Year2026
ScopeDevelopment, architecture, visualization
Concept

Aether is designed as a restrained island house that prioritizes atmosphere over spectacle. The architecture frames the horizon while remaining grounded in the topography and material language of the Cyclades.

The project is organized around exterior living, sheltered thresholds and a direct relationship between interior rooms, terrace, pool and landscape. Its expression is intentionally quiet: stone, plaster, shade, water and light.

Private residence

Corfu Residence

A private residence renovation in Corfu, completed in 1994. A layered family project combining architectural intervention, long-term ownership and the careful transformation of an existing home.

LocationCorfu, Greece
TypologyDuplex penthouse
Area160 m²
StatusCompleted
Year1994
ScopeArchitecture & Interiors
Approach

Located in the historic centre of Corfu, this duplex penthouse was comprehensively redeveloped between 1992 and 1994. The project combines traditional urban fabric with generous interior volumes, exposed timber roof structures and panoramic views towards the sea and the Old Fortress. Designed as a long-term family residence, the project reflects a commitment to durable materials, craftsmanship and timeless architectural character rather than short-term trends.

Its inclusion in the APXR portfolio marks the continuity between earlier family-led architectural work and the company’s current direction: improving assets with restraint, long-term thinking and respect for place.
